The problem

Drowning in CVs
every time you hire?

Recruiters handle multiple roles at once. Each new job brings a flood of CVs – and hardly enough time to review them.

Once the role is filled, most profiles are forgotten – even the good ones.

The solution

Let AI do the
CV reading for you

Just upload a CV – Selekt instantly summarizes the candidate, highlights key skills, and organizes the data.

No need for formatting or copy-pasting – it’s structured, fast, and fully automated.

The long-term win

All candidates saved, sorted and searchable.

Next time you recruit, just search your AI-sorted database.

Filter by skills, role or experience – and instantly rediscover great candidates you already have.
